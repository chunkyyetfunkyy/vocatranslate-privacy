Common Issues

Login Problems

  • Account locked: After 5 failed login attempts, accounts are permanently locked. Contact your administrator to unlock your account.
  • First login: All new accounts require a password change on first login. Follow the on-screen prompts to create a secure password.

Translation Issues

  • No internet connection: VocaTranslate requires an active internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular) for translation services.
  • Microphone access: Ensure VocaTranslate has microphone permissions enabled in Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone.
  • Speech recognition: Speak clearly and at a normal pace. Background noise can affect recognition accuracy.

Session Timeout

  • Sessions automatically timeout after 15 minutes of inactivity for security compliance.
  • Simply log back in to resume. Your language settings are preserved.
